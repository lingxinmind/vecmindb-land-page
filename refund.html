30-Day Money-Back Guarantee / 30天无条件退款保证

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all VecminDB paid subscription licenses (Pro and Team plans). If you are not completely satisfied with our service, you may request a full refund within 30 calendar days of your initial purchase date.

我们为所有 VecminDB 付费订阅许可（Pro 和 Team 套餐）提供 30 天无条件退款保证。如果您对我们的服务不完全满意，您可以在首次购买之日起 30 个日历天内申请全额退款。

Subscription Cancellation / 订阅取消与自动续费

Paid monthly and annual subscriptions can be canceled at any time through your local license management panel or by contacting billing support. Upon cancellation, you will continue to have full write access to the database engine and API services until the end of your current active billing cycle. No further automatic renewals will occur thereafter.

付费的月度及年度订阅均可随时通过您的本地许可管理控制面板取消，或联系我们的账单支持团队处理。取消后，您将继续享有完整的数据库引擎写入访问和 API 服务，直至当前计费周期结束。此后系统将不再发起自动续费。

Refund Request Process / 退款申诉处理流程

To request a refund, please send an email to our official billing team at sulingqi@hotmail.com. Your email must include your registered email address, account identifier, and the transaction receipt. Once approved, the refund will be processed back to your original payment method (via Stripe, 2Checkout or other authorized payment gateway gateways) within 5 to 10 business days.

如需申请退款，请发送电子邮件至我们的官方账单支持邮箱：sulingqi@hotmail.com。您的邮件需包含注册邮箱、账户标识符及交易凭证。一旦审核通过，退款将在 5 至 10 个工作日内，原路退回到您原有的支付渠道（通过 Stripe、2Checkout 或其他授权网关）。
